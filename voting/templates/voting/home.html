❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📌 How do I vote?
Login with your registration number, select a position from the dropdown menu, review the candidates, make your selection, and submit. You can vote for multiple positions, one at a time.
🔑 What if I forgot my registration number?
Contact the election support team at apsharas2005@nec.edu.in or visit the student affairs office for assistance with your registration number.
🔄 Can I change my vote after submitting?
No, votes are final once submitted for each position. Please review your choice carefully before submitting your vote.
🕵️ Is my vote anonymous?
Yes! Your vote is completely anonymous. The system only records that you voted for a particular position, not who you voted for.
📅 When will results be announced?
Results will be announced after the voting period closes on October 11, 2025. Admins can view live results at any time.
📱 Can I vote from my mobile phone?
Yes! The voting system is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers.
